Introduction

In an era where technology is advancing rapidly into many fields, especially with the rise of artificial intelligence, there is a growing opportunity for an innovative, personalized approach to address personal health concerns. Our project intends to take advantage of these new technologies to provide personalized health assistance in the form of a chatbot, which will provide users with guidance regarding their health in an intuitive conversational format. With the prevalence of tech in people's lives becoming more normalized, a chatbot for personal health is a natural step for the future of healthcare. Our chatbot is intended to be capable of covering multiple health-related topics with its users, including symptoms, wellness tips, fitness routines, nutrition advice, and mental health support, in a conversational, friendly, and supportive manner.Currently, healthcare in the United States faces several challenges, including limited access to personal health information and overwhelmed systems. Additionally, with the plethora of information online, it can be overwhelming and lacking in what would be best for individuals. Our chatbot will bridge the gap between the data and the individual in an innovative, personalized way that will fix many of these problems.

Next we have the Usefulness and Impact of the Chatbot. First of, the Chatbot provides Instant Access to Information. Instead of taking time to set up a meeting with a doctor, waiting, and then meeting with this doctor, which can waste an immense amount of time, our chatbot will give immediate and reliable access to reliable health information without any time in between. It gives personal guidance. Unlike a typical doctor who has to worry about many people at a time, our chatbot‘s sole focus is the user, and its guidance (based on user data) is tuned to the needs of the individual. It also provides Preventive Care. Our chatbot will offer proactive suggestions to its users to maintain good health, preventing problems instead of just fixing them. Finally it empowers users, Users of our chatbot will take an active approach to their health and well-being, and see positive changes from the suggestions of our chatbot.
